— Who we are

Expertise rooted
in the dunes

With over six years navigating the untamed landscapes of Namibia, Michael and the Infinite African Safaris team provide unparalleled access to the region's hidden gems.

Locally owned and based in Walvis Bay, we craft every journey as a personal narrative — family-friendly, accessible to guests with disabilities, and always small-group. Our guides hold valid Professional Driving Permits and First-Aid certification, so every kilometre is safe, informative and genuinely fun.
