— Around the fire

Braai schools

In Namibia the braai isn't dinner — it's the whole evening. Learn to cook over open coals the local way: the fire, the cuts, the timing, the stories. You leave with a full plate and a skill for life.

  1. 01Light the coals

    Build and master a proper wood-and-charcoal fire the Namibian way.

  2. 02Master the cuts

    Boerewors, kapana, lamb chops and the day's fresh catch — seasoned and grilled to the second.

  3. 03Feast under the stars

    Sit down to your own braai as the desert sky lights up. Drinks and stories included.
